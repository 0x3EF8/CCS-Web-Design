Our Direction

What We Stand For

Our mission and vision define every decision we make — from curriculum design to the way we mentor each student on their journey.

Mission

Delivering Excellence in Computing Education

The College of Computing Studies is committed to providing high-quality, outcomes-based computing education that equips students with the technical expertise, analytical mindset, and professional ethics required to thrive in the global technology industry.

We cultivate an environment of continuous learning, research-driven inquiry, and community engagement — preparing graduates who are not only skilled practitioners but also responsible digital citizens.

Vision

A Premier Hub for Computing Excellence in the Region

We envision the College of Computing Studies as a recognized center of academic excellence and technological innovation — producing world-class computing professionals who contribute meaningfully to national development and global progress.

By 2030, CCS aims to be ranked among the top performing computing departments in the region, distinguished by its research output, industry partnerships, and graduate impact.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Progress

From a small computing unit to a full-fledged department, our story is one of continuous growth, resilience, and dedication to academic excellence.

2004

Department Established

The College of Computing Studies was formally established with an initial batch of 48 students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology — the beginning of CCS as a standalone academic unit.

2008

BSCS Program Launched

CCS expanded its academic offerings with the introduction of the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program, allowing students to pursue deeper specialization in algorithms, theory, and software engineering.

2013

First Regional Hackathon Champions

CCS students claimed first place at the Regional Intercollegiate Programming Contest, putting the department on the competitive computing map and validating its rigorous curriculum and hands-on training approach.

2018

Level III Accreditation Achieved

Both BSCS and BSIT programs were awarded Level III Accreditation — the highest recognition for academic quality in the region — affirming the department's commitment to excellence in instruction and research.

2022

Industry Partnership Program Launched

CCS formalized partnerships with 25+ regional and national tech companies, creating direct pathways for student internships, capstone industry sponsors, and a structured job placement pipeline for graduating seniors.

2025

Record Enrollment & 98% Employment

CCS reached its highest enrollment in history with over 1,200 active students, while the Class of 2025 recorded a 98% employment rate within six months of graduation — the best outcome in the department's history.
