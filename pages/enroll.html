Enrollment Process

How to Enroll in 5 Steps

Follow this straightforward process to secure your place in the CCS Department. Each step is designed to be completed quickly and conveniently online.

  1. 01

    Submit Online Application

    Visit the CCS Admissions Portal and fill out the online application form. Choose your desired program (BSCS or BSIT), provide personal and academic information, and upload a recent 2×2 ID photo. The form takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.

    Online Form Program Selection Photo Upload
  2. 02

    Upload Required Documents

    Log in to the portal and upload scanned copies of all required documents. Ensure files are clear, legible, and in PDF or JPG format. Incomplete submissions will be returned for correction — double-check the requirements checklist before submitting.

    PDF / JPG Format Clear & Legible Verification 24 hrs
  3. 03

    Take the Entrance Examination

    After document verification, you will receive a confirmation email with your exam schedule. The CCS Admission Exam covers Mathematics, English proficiency, Abstract Reasoning, and basic Computing Aptitude. The exam is offered both online and on-campus.

    Mathematics English Proficiency Computing Aptitude
  4. 04

    Attend Program Orientation & Interview

    Qualified applicants are invited to a program orientation session where you will meet faculty, learn about curriculum expectations, and participate in a brief personal interview. This helps us understand your goals and ensures the right program fit for your background.

    Meet Faculty Brief Interview Online / On-campus
  5. 05

    Pay Fees & Confirm Enrollment

    Upon acceptance, pay your enrollment fees through any accredited payment channel (GCash, bank transfer, over-the-counter). Once payment is confirmed, you will receive your student ID number, section assignment, and class schedule via your registered email.

    GCash / Bank Student ID Issued Schedule Released
Admission Requirements

What You Need to Prepare

Gather these documents before starting your application to ensure a smooth and complete submission. Originals may be required upon final enrollment.

Incoming Freshmen

New Students

  • Form 138 / SF9 — Grade 12 Report Card (original)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate (original or certified copy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (from school principal)
  • 2×2 ID Photos — 4 copies, white background
  • Medical & Dental Certificate (from licensed physician)
  • Senior High School Diploma / Certificate of Completion
  • Accomplished Application Form (downloadable from portal)
Bring 1 photocopy of each document alongside the originals on enrollment day.
From Another School

Transferees

  • Honorable Dismissal from previous institution
  • Official Transcript of Records (OTR) — certified true copy
  • Course Description / Syllabus of previously taken subjects
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate (original or certified copy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character from previous school
  • 2×2 ID Photos — 4 copies, white background
  • Medical & Dental Certificate (from licensed physician)
Credit evaluation is conducted by the Dean's Office within 5 business days of submission.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions prospective students ask before and during the enrollment process.

The exam is designed to assess aptitude, not prior computing knowledge. It covers high-school-level math, logical reasoning, and English comprehension. A score of 70% or above is typically required for admission.

CCS accepts approximately 120 students per program (BSCS and BSIT) per year, divided across two sections. Slots are awarded strictly on the basis of exam results and documentary completeness.

Program shifting is allowed after completing at least one semester. Applicants must file a shifting form, meet the minimum GPA requirement of 2.50, and obtain department head approval. Shifted units are evaluated for credit transfer.

Yes. CCS offers CHED scholarships, institutional merit grants, and the government's UNIFAST Tertiary Education Subsidy (TES). Additional industry-sponsored scholarships are available through our partner companies. Visit the Scholarships Office for details.

You may submit a Conditional Enrollment request along with a written explanation and expected availability date. Conditional enrollees must complete all requirements within 30 days of the enrollment period or risk losing their slot.
