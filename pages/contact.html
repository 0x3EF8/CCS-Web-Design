FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we receive most often.

Email inquiries are typically answered within 1–2 business days. Urgent concerns may be directed to our department office by phone during office hours for immediate assistance.

Yes. Prospective students may request an official campus tour through the Admissions Office. Tours are available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM by appointment only.

Physical documents may be submitted to the CCS Admissions Office (Room CCS-101, Ground Floor) during office hours. Scanned copies may also be uploaded via the online admissions portal.

Scholarship inquiries should be directed to the CCS Admissions Office or the University Scholarships and Financial Aid Office. Email ccs.admissions@university.edu.ph with “Scholarship Inquiry” as the subject line.

Yes. Faculty members and department officers are available for online consultations via video call by prior appointment. Request an online meeting slot through the Admissions Office email.

Industry and alumni partnership proposals are handled by the Dean’s Office. Email ccs.dean@university.edu.ph with “Partnership Proposal” as the subject. We welcome collaborations from CCS alumni and industry partners.
