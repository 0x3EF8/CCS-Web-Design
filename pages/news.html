College of Computer Studies — Shaping the Next Generation of IT Professionals

The College of Computer Studies at Saint Joseph College, Maasin City has been at the forefront of technology education in Southern Leyte for over 20 years. Offering three CHED-recognized programs — BSCS, BSIT, and the Associate in Computer Technology (ACT-CS & ACT-IT) — the department is home to 14+ dedicated faculty members, 14 PhilNITS passers, and a growing community of students committed to excellence in computing and information technology.

CCS Now Offers 3 Programs: BSCS, BSIT & Associate in Computer Technology

The College of Computer Studies at Saint Joseph College offers three academic programs — giving students flexible pathways into the technology field. The Associate in Computer Technology (ACT) is the foundational 2-year program: ACT-CS leads to BSCS, and ACT-IT leads to BSIT, before students progress into the full four-year degrees.
