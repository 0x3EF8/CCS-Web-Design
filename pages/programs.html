Requirements

Admission Requirements

Open to all incoming freshmen and transferees who meet the academic and entrance requirements of the college.

01
Academic Credentials
  • Form 138 / Senior High School Report Card
  • Grade 12 certificate of graduation
  • PSA-authenticated birth certificate
  • 2×2 ID photo (4 pieces)
02
Entrance Examination
  • CCS College Entrance Test (CET)
  • Coverage: Math, Logic & Critical Thinking, Basic Computing
  • Passing score: 70% or higher
  • Exam schedules: March — April each year
03
For Transferees
  • Transcript of Records (TOR) from previous school
  • Honorable Dismissal / Transfer Credentials
  • At least 75% general average in all CS/IT subjects
  • No failing grades in major subjects
04
Enrollment Steps
  • Submit requirements to the Registrar’s Office
  • Take and pass the CCS Entrance Test
  • Receive Notice of Acceptance (NOA)
  • Pay enrollment fees & confirm slot

Limited slots are available each academic year. Early application is strongly encouraged. For questions, contact our department online.
