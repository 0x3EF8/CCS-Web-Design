Dr. Maria L. Santos, PhD

Dean — College of Computing Studies

Dr. Santos has over 22 years of experience in computing education and research. She earned her Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science from De La Salle University, with a dissertation focused on adaptive learning systems and computational intelligence. Under her leadership, CCS has been recognized as a CHED Center of Development and has forged partnerships with leading technology companies across the Philippines and Southeast Asia.
